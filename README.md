# BWTV-Composite-2541
Composite mod for Black and White TVs with TDA2540/2541 Jungle Chip

This is a quick and dirty composite-amplifier circuit to get a composite signal to the output level of a TDA2540 (or TDA2541)

How to install:

source 12V power from the TV 

cut the trace on the pin 12 of TDA2540

connect the output of the circuit, not on pin 12 of the TDA2540, but on the other side of the cut trace

Enjoy yout freshly modded composite TV
